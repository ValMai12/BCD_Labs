Spanish architecture:

Famous Buildings of Spain

Spain is famous for its diverse architectural styles, including Gothic, Islamic, and Modernism. Many Spanish buildings are considered masterpieces of world heritage.

Navigation:

  • Gothic
  • Islamic
  • Modernism

One of the most iconic landmarks is the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona, designed by Antoni Gaudí.

Another outstanding monument is the Alhambra in Granada, which represents the beauty of Islamic architecture.

But my personal favorite is Casa Batlló, which is also the work of Antoni Gaudí.

I especially love its roof, because it resembles a dragon.
